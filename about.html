About

Hi, my name is Ashlynn!I am currently a junior in high school, with dreams of becoming a traveling doctor.

I have been interested in medicine, current events, and helping people since I was child.

I created Microchanges to shed light on the inaccessibility women and children in particular have to healthcare.

I would like to bring awareness to the Zika outbreak, as well as help rectify the lack of proper, functioning healthcare settings in developing countries.
